# The Survey Component

The `<Survey>` primitive allows you to aggressively gather qualitative and quantitative telemetry from your users without requiring them to leave your app to fill out a Google Form. 

## Usage & Strategy

Surveys are unique because they are typically gate-kept by prior completion metrics. You should not spam users with Surveys upon login.

Instead, map the `<Survey>` component to trigger *after* a specific, meaningful interaction has successfully completed.

- **Event-Driven Rollouts**: Trigger a Survey immediately after a user successfully exports a CSV using your newly launched Reports Engine.
- **Short-Form Focus**: In-app surveys must be frictionless. Keep them to 1-2 questions maximum.
- **Graceful Deferment**: FeatureDrop Survey components include built-in "Ask Me Later" semantics, which map to configurable cooldown windows in your Storage Adapter.

<Callout type="tip">
  **NPS Campaigns**: The Survey component natively supports 1-10 range inputs for Net Promoter Score (NPS) campaigns or 1-5 star ratings for CSAT scorecards.
</Callout>
