# The Spotlight Chain

The `<SpotlightChain>` component takes the isolation mechanics of a standard Spotlight and sequences them together into a guided, multi-step workflow.

## Usage & Strategy

If a massive workflow redesign just launched and you need to show users how three distinct areas of the dashboard now collaborate, a Spotlight Chain is your best tool.

- **Sequential Discovery**: It allows you to highlight `Target A`, and only once the user completes the action (or clicks "Next" in the tooltip), does the overlay shift smoothly to illuminate `Target B`.
- **Alternative to Tours**: Spotlight Chains are a modern, highly-visual alternative to standard `<Tour>` Modals, requiring far less reading and enforcing much more contextual awareness.

<Callout type="info">
  **Routing Interoperability**: In complex Enterprise applications, a Spotlight Chain can persist across React Router or Next.js page navigations. For example, Step 1 can highlight an item on `/dashboard`, and when clicked, Step 2 will seamlessly illuminate a different element on the resulting `/settings` page.
</Callout>
